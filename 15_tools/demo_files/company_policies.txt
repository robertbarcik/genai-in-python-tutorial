1. REMOTE WORK POLICY
---------------------

TechStart Inc. supports flexible work arrangements. Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Requirements for remote work:
- Stable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps recommended)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Availability during core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time
- Participation in all scheduled video meetings with camera on

Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance. Managers have discretion to approve or deny requests based on team needs and project deadlines.

Fully remote positions are available for certain roles. These positions are clearly marked in job postings and require explicit approval from the department head.

2. VACATION AND TIME OFF
------------------------

Annual Leave Allocation:
- 0-2 years of service: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years of service: 20 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 25 days per year

Sick Leave:
- All employees receive 10 sick days per year
- Unused sick days do not roll over to the next year
- Doctor's note required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days

Public Holidays:
- TechStart observes 10 public holidays per year
- Holiday calendar is published in December for the following year

Parental Leave:
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks paid leave
- Additional unpaid leave up to 12 weeks available upon request

Vacation requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for periods of 3+ days. Same-day requests may be approved for emergencies at manager's discretion.

3. EQUIPMENT AND SOFTWARE
-------------------------

Standard Equipment Package:
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS, based on role requirements)
- External monitor (24" minimum)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls

Software Licenses Provided:
- Microsoft 365 (Outlook, Teams, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- Slack for internal communication
- Zoom for external meetings
- GitHub for development teams
- Figma for design teams
- Salesforce for sales teams

Equipment Refresh Cycle:
- Laptops are refreshed every 3 years
- Monitors and peripherals refreshed every 5 years
- Employees may request early refresh with justification

Personal Device Policy:
- Personal devices may be used for work with IT approval
- Mobile Device Management (MDM) software must be installed
- Company data must not be stored on personal devices

4. SECURITY GUIDELINES
----------------------

Password Requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Password rotation every 90 days
- No password reuse for last 10 passwords

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
- Required for all company systems
- Approved methods: Authenticator app, hardware key
- SMS-based 2FA is not permitted

Data Classification:
- Public: Information that can be freely shared
- Internal: For employees only, not for external sharing
- Confidential: Sensitive business information, need-to-know basis
- Restricted: Highly sensitive data, requires explicit authorization

Incident Reporting:
- Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery
- Contact: security@techstart.com or call +1-555-SEC-RITY
- Do not attempt to investigate incidents independently

5. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
------------------------

Eligible Expenses:
- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Client meals and entertainment (with prior approval)
- Professional development and training
- Home office equipment (up to $500/year for remote workers)

Submission Process:
- All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase
- Receipts required for expenses over $25
- Submit through the Expense Management System (EMS)
- Manager approval required for expenses over $100

Travel Guidelines:
- Book flights at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours
- Hotel budget: up to $200/night in major cities, $150/night elsewhere

Per Diem Rates:
- Domestic travel: $75/day for meals
- International travel: Varies by country (see HR portal)

Reimbursement Timeline:
- Approved expenses are reimbursed within 10 business days
- Direct deposit to employee's payroll account
